1 Introduction

Welcome to TapBudget ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the TapBudget expense tracking and budgeting application on iOS mobile devices.

By using TapBudget, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the application.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: Your email address, display name, and profile picture when authenticating via Google or Apple Sign In.
  • Budget data: Monthly income targets, savings goals, and fixed expense categories you set up during onboarding.
  • Transaction data: Dates, amounts, descriptions, and categories of expenses and income you log manually or via the Apple Shortcut integration.
  • API Key: A unique, randomly generated identifier assigned to your account for authenticating Apple Shortcut requests. This key is stored in our database and can be regenerated at any time from the Profile tab.
  • Device and usage information: We may collect general, non-identifying technical information (such as app version and operating system version) for the purpose of diagnosing bugs and improving the app.
🔒 We do not collect credit card details, financial account numbers, or any payment information. TapBudget does not have a paid subscription model and does not process payments.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve TapBudget. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Create and manage your user account and authenticate your identity.
  • Store your budget settings and sync them across your devices when you are signed in.
  • Receive, validate, and securely relay transactions submitted via the Apple Shortcut integration to your device.
  • Validate API Key requests to ensure only you can log transactions to your account.
  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access, fraud, or misuse of the Service.
  • Respond to your support requests and communications.
  • Improve the performance, reliability, and features of TapBudget.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal or financial data to third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies under any circumstances.

4 Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Transaction history is stored locally on your device. It is not permanently stored on our servers. If you delete the app or switch devices, this local data cannot be recovered.
  • Budget settings and account data are stored securely in an encrypted cloud database (Supabase) to allow syncing when you are signed in.
  • Shortcut transactions are temporarily stored on our servers only during transit. They are permanently and automatically deleted from our servers as soon as they are confirmed as synced to your device.
  • All data transmission between the app, our API, and our database is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

No method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we implement commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5 Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate TapBudget. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

  • Apple (Sign In with Apple & Apple Shortcuts): Used for authentication and the back-tap shortcut integration. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Google Sign In): Used as an optional authentication method. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Used for secure cloud database storage and user authentication infrastructure. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your information.

✏️

You can exercise the right to delete your account directly within the app by navigating to Profile → Account Actions → Delete Account. For all other data requests, please contact us at TapBudget@outlook.com.

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Account and budget data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, this data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Shortcut transaction data is deleted from our servers automatically within minutes of being synced to your device, and no later than 24 hours after submission.
  • Local transaction history on your device is retained until you manually clear it, delete the app, or request account deletion.

8 Children's Privacy

TapBudget is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at TapBudget@outlook.com and we will promptly delete that information.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of TapBudget after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
