Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

Welcome to TapBudget ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the TapBudget mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your email address and basic profile information (like your name) provided by your authentication provider (e.g., Apple, Google).
  • Financial Data: We store your budget settings (monthly income, savings goals, and fixed expenses) securely in our database to sync across your devices.
  • Transaction History: By default, your daily transaction history is stored locally on your device. When using the Apple Shortcut integration, transactions are temporarily passed through our secure servers and are immediately deleted from the server once synced to your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve the TapBudget app. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power our infrastructure:

  • Supabase: For secure database hosting and user authentication.
  • Vercel: For hosting our web endpoints.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the TapBudget app by navigating to the Profile tab and selecting "Delete Account".

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at TapBudget@outlook.com.
